RES·O·NANCE

noun

Physics: the reinforcement or prolongation of sound by reflection or by the synchronous vibration of a neighboring object.
When one string vibrates at a certain frequency, another nearby string begins to hum in response—without being touched.
Emotional/Conceptual: the power to evoke strong emotion or meaning through deep alignment or familiarity.
When a word, idea, or memory “rings true,” creating a sense of profound connection.
Resonance in this Context

Something it says aligns so precisely with something deep inside you, it creates a physical, emotional, or symbolic response.
